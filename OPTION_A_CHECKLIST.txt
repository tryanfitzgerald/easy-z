  2. Look for PDF or zoning page
  3. Copy URL
  4. Write it down above

TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK ANSWERS

Q: Can't find URL for a city?
A: That's OK. Try the fallback searches mentioned in the guide. Or skip it - the 
   system will try to find it through other strategies.

Q: Found multiple URLs for one city?
A: Pick the most recent one (usually marked "current"). Direct PDFs are best.

Q: Is Municode URL OK?
A: Yes! Municode URLs work great.

Q: How do I know URL is correct?
A: If it contains "zoning", "ordinance", or "code" and is from the city website, 
   you're good.
